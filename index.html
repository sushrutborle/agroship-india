About AgroShip India

A Pune-based agri export startup built by professionals

Our Story

Built by Experts, For Global Markets

AgroShip India is a Pune-based agricultural export company combining deep supply chain engineering expertise with chartered accountancy-backed compliance to deliver premium Indian produce to global buyers.

Founded with a vision to connect India's rich agricultural heartland — from Nashik's onion fields to Sangli's turmeric markets — with buyers across the UAE, UK, USA and Southeast Asia.

We handle everything: APEDA-compliant documentation, phytosanitary certificates, cold chain logistics and FOB pricing — so your import is seamless.
